\# AI Command Center - System Architecture



\## 1. Product Direction



AI Command Center is a SaaS-based AI Customer Engagement Platform.



The platform should support multiple organizations. Each organization can connect its own social channels, knowledge base, users, leads, customers, workflows and AI settings.



The SAP Guru is the first organization/workspace.
