\# AI Command Center - Product Roadmap



\## Product Vision



AI Command Center is a SaaS-based AI Customer Engagement Platform that enables organizations to manage customer conversations, automate engagement, qualify leads, assist sales teams, and provide customer intelligence across multiple communication channels.



The platform must remain industry-agnostic. Industry-specific knowledge should be injected through organization-specific knowledge bases and configurations.
